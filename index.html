Our Service Offerings in Detail

üåø Ecoscape by PlotNXT

Creation & Green Infrastructure (One-Time Setup)

Ecoscape projects are designed to convert raw or underutilized land into functional, plant-ready green spaces. Each project is approached as a one-time creation exercise, tailored to the site's soil condition, layout, access, and intended long-term use.

How Ecoscape Works

1. Site Assessment & Green Layout Planning

Plot evaluation, soil review, slope and drainage understanding, and functional layout planning.

2. Ground Preparation & Infrastructure Creation

Soil preparation, pit digging, raised beds, irrigation routing, and base infrastructure.

3. Plantation & Establishment

Plantation of trees, vegetables, lawns, or forest patches based on the agreed plan.

4. Handover for Ongoing Care (Optional)

Once the green foundation is established, the plot can be transitioned to GreenCare for ongoing management.

Typical Ecoscape Projects

  • Plot preparation for long-term green use
  • Raised vegetable beds and irrigation setup
  • Lawns and forest patches
  • Rooftop and terrace gardens
  • Green layout planning for new or idle plots

Ecoscape projects are custom-designed. Scope, timelines, and pricing are defined after site assessment.
