Ecoscape by PlotNXT

Creation & Green Infrastructure (One-Time Setup)

Ecoscape is PlotNXT's design-and-build vertical responsible for creating the green foundation on a plot. It covers all one-time and project-based work required to establish functional, plant-ready green spaces.

  • Soil preparation and pit digging
  • Raised vegetable beds
  • Plantation and drip irrigation systems
  • Lawns and forest patches
  • Rooftop gardens
  • Green layout planning

Ecoscape creates the foundation for sustainable green living.

GreenCare by PlotNXT

Managed Monthly Green Services (Subscription)

GreenCare is PlotNXT's subscription-based service for ongoing care, management, and productivity of green assets. After infrastructure is created, trained farmer couples take responsibility for maintaining plant health, cleanliness, and overall usability of the plot.

  • Regular visits and plant care
  • Vegetable and fruit management
  • Pruning and watering supervision
  • Seasonal upkeep
  • Trained farmer couples
  • Four service tiers available

GreenCare manages, nurtures, and sustains what Ecoscape creates.

GreenOnCall by PlotNXT

On-Demand Garden & Plot Services

GreenOnCall is PlotNXT's on-demand service for customers who require specific gardening or plot-related work without a subscription. It is suitable for seasonal cleanups, pruning, bed preparation, plantation support, audits, or special requirements.

  • Pruning and bed preparation
  • Plantation support
  • Garden audits
  • Special requirements
  • Same quality standards
